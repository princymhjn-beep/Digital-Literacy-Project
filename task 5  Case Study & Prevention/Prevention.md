## **Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist**

**“Stay Safe Online” – Prevention Tips for College Students**

1. **Verify URLs** before clicking links in emails or messages; official apps rarely send login links via WhatsApp.  
2. **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on all accounts for extra security.  
3. **Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs** with anyone, even if they claim to be bank staff. ✅  
4. **Use official UPI apps** and avoid third-party links for transactions. ✅  
5. **Update devices and apps** regularly to patch security vulnerabilities.  
6. **Be cautious with public Wi-Fi**, avoid logging into banking or personal accounts there.  
7. **Report suspicious messages or transactions** immediately via the **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)** or **Helpline 1930**.  
8. **Educate peers** about phishing and online scams; share trusted resources.
