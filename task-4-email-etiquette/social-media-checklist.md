**\#\# \*\*Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts\*\***

**\*\*Do's\*\***

\* Post content respectfully and responsibly.    
\* Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.    
\* Share educational or positive content.    
\* Verify facts before sharing information.    
\* Interact professionally with peers and colleagues.

**\*\*Don'ts\*\***

\* Share personal passwords or sensitive information.    
\* Post inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content.    
\* Engage in online arguments or trolling.    
\* Over-share personal details publicly.    
\* Accept connection requests from unknown or suspicious profiles.
